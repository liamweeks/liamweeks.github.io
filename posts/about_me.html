About Me

Hi, I’m Liam — a computer science student and software developer with a deep interest in solving problems with code. I thrive in that sweet spot between elegant abstraction and practical implementation.

My background includes building hardware projects like an 8-bit computer from 74LS chips, developing web apps, creating machine learning pipelines, and experimenting with cryptographic protocols. I especially enjoy challenges that combine systems thinking, security, and math.

Outside of tech, I play rugby, volunteer in my community, and occasionally teach kids how to build robots or write their first lines of code.

This blog is where I explore side projects, reflect on learning experiences, and sometimes share puzzles I’m working on. Thanks for reading!
